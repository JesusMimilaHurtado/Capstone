Budi's Bakery

Budi's Bakery was founded in April of 2024. It began as an imaginary online store. A project to help me sharpen my skills and increase my familiarity with frontend coding. Using critical thinking, HTML, CSS, and some bootstrap this project was brought to life. A bakery selling baked goods from different nations.

As a person of a diverse background I like to celebrate and enjoy all cultures and food. Which is why i created this to bring taste from all around together. Some these baked goods we sell are from:

  • Europe,
  • the Middle East,
  • and Latin countries.

Our Mission Satement

To get locally sourced produce and make fresh baked goods. Making it easy to find high quality bread and pastries for YOU TO ENJOY!
