About Us

We are a team of passionate bakers with a love for diversity and baked goods. Our bakery is more than just a place to pick up a loaf of bread, pastry or a sweet treat; it's a community, where memories are made and flavors come alive. Baking isn't just our profession; it's our passion. From the crack of dawn, our team of dedicated bakers meticulously crafts each pastry, bread, and cake with love and care, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally whenever possible.

Mission Statement

To get locally sourced produce and make fresh baked goods. Making it easy to find high quality bread and pastries for YOU TO ENJOY!
